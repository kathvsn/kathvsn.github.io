HOW TO PLAY 

----------------------

  • The objective of the game is to shop for breakfast, lunch, and dinner!
  • Remember you only have $50 to spend, so choose your items wisely!
  • You can earn points too! Each aisle has a set amount of points, but you won't know which aisle has the most points!
  • For every dollar you save, half a point will be added to your total score!
  • At the end of the game you can check out how much you spent and saved!
