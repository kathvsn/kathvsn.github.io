Inspiration  

Last year I took on bullet journaling as a hobby, and I was inspired by this to create a digital diary for my final website where I can log my daily thoughts, activities and images. Bullet journaling a method of journaling and note-taking that uses bullet points as the core structure. The main idea behind the bullet journal is that you jot down quick notes instead of writing long sentences. It is a combination between a diary, a planner, and a to-do list.
